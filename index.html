Video Echoed in Music:
Semantic-Temporal Video Parsing Guided Latent Music Diffusion for Video-to-Music Generation

Welcome to Video Demo site for the paper. A collection of 5 distinct videos with 6 different music generated by four comparative methods (VidMuse, M2UGen, Diff-bgm, and CMT) and the proposed approach alongside the groundtruth. The video samples are from the validation set of the proposed novel VM-Ads dataset.
